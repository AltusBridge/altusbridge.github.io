Co-Investment Platform

Invest alongside Altus Bridge

Altus Bridge operates a deal-by-deal co-investment platform for sophisticated investors seeking direct access to India-linked private credit, infrastructure, real assets, and alternative investment opportunities.

Each deal is structured via a GIFT City or Mauritius SPV, with Altus Bridge as deal lead. Investors participate alongside institutional capital with clearly defined entry terms, security, and exit horizon.

Target investors: GCC family offices · Singapore institutions · African HNWIs · NRIs under LRS · Domestic Indian family offices.

Express interest →
01

Deal sourced & structured

Altus Bridge originates, conducts due diligence, and structures the SPV — GIFT City IBU or Mauritius GBL depending on LP geography.

02

Soft-circle & LP onboarding

Deal brief shared with pre-qualified LPs. Commitments collected, KYC completed via admin partner. Subscription documents executed.

03

SPV closes & capital deployed

Capital flows into target via structured instrument — NCD, secured loan, or equity co-invest. Security created and registered.

04

Quarterly reporting & exit

LP updates via fund administrator. Exit at liquidity event — IPO, PE secondary, strategic sale, or debt repayment.

Cross-Border Payments

Seamless capital movement across borders

Practical payment infrastructure for fund managers, family offices, and corporates operating across India, GCC, Africa, and Southeast Asia.

Multi-Currency Accounts

USD, GBP, EUR, AED, SGD, and INR accounts for family offices and HNIs. Hold, convert, and send across 40+ currencies without the friction of traditional banking. Ideal for GCC and Singapore investors accessing India opportunities.

Cross-Border Remittance

Fast, compliant cross-border payments for businesses operating across the India–GCC–Africa corridor. Trade settlements, inter-company transfers, and supplier payments at institutional FX rates without correspondent banking delays.

Fund Payment Infrastructure

Capital call processing, LP distribution management, and multi-currency fund accounting for AIF managers and SPV administrators. Integrates with GIFT City IBU and Mauritius fund banking to streamline the full capital lifecycle.

Payment solutions are delivered in partnership with regulated FCA, DFSA, and MAS-compliant payment providers. Altus Bridge acts as an introduction and advisory intermediary — not as a regulated payment institution. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements.
