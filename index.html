About

Abhishek Mohta

I am an independent capital advisory professional with 15+ years across investment banking, debt capital markets, fund structuring, and cross-border finance.

My practice — Altus Bridge — focuses on three things: structuring the right fund vehicle, finding the right capital, and navigating the regulatory and jurisdictional complexity in between.

I work with AIF fund managers, family offices, HNIs, and corporates exploring fund platforms, infrastructure financing, and cross-border capital deployment — across the Global South corridor spanning India, West Asia, Africa, and Southeast Asia.

Prior to building Altus Bridge, I held roles at Natixis, HDFC Bank, Essar Group, Essar Ports, Amicorp, and Capwise Financial Services — with deal exposure across renewable energy, ports, infrastructure, and real assets spanning APAC, Sub-Saharan Africa, DIFC, GIFT City, Mauritius, and Luxembourg.
